# About

## Michal Elias

I am a researcher and developer with a background in geodesy and Earth observation,
specialising in the statistical analysis of scientific time series. My work combines
domain expertise in GNSS and atmospheric science with software development in C++,
Python, and MATLAB.

---

## Research Interests

**Geodesy and satellite navigation**

- Advanced and satellite geodesy
- GNSS positioning and error modelling
- Draconitic signals and periodic effects in GNSS coordinate time series

**Atmospheric science**

- Numerical modelling of tropospheric delays
- Integrated Water Vapour (IWV) estimation from GNSS
- Ionospheric delay modelling

**Data analysis and statistics**

- Time series analysis: homogeneity testing, change point detection, outlier removal
- Statistical signal processing: spectral analysis, decomposition, SSA, Kalman filtering
- Extreme Value Theory for reliability and safety analysis
- Spatial interpolation: Kriging and geostatistics
