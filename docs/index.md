# Michal Elias

**Researcher · Data Analyst · Developer**

---

I am a researcher and software developer with over ten years of experience in geodesy,
GNSS meteorology, and scientific data analysis. My work sits at the intersection of
satellite geodesy, atmospheric science, and applied statistics — building tools and
methods that extract reliable information from noisy, real-world observational data.

Currently I develop **LOKI**, a modular C++20 framework for statistical analysis of
scientific time series, with applications in climatology, GNSS tropospheric delays,
and vehicle sensor data.

---

## Research Interests

- GNSS and GNSS meteorology
- Numerical modelling of tropospheric and ionospheric delays
- Advanced and satellite geodesy
- Time series analysis and change point detection
- Signal processing and mathematical statistics
