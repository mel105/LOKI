Detailed Description

Kriging estimate at a single target point.

In temporal mode the target is identified by its MJD value. FUTURE (spatial): add x, y coordinate fields.

The Kriging variance sigma^2 quantifies prediction uncertainty. CI bounds assume a Gaussian distribution: CI = value +/- z * sqrt(variance) where z is the N(0,1) quantile at the configured confidence level.

With a nugget effect, variance at observation locations equals the nugget (not zero), reflecting measurement noise that is always present.
