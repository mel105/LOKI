Detailed Description

Computes and stores a median annual profile from a TimeSeries.

The profile is a flat vector of length 366 * slotsPerDay, where each slot holds the median of all valid values observed at that position (day-of-year

  • time-of-day bucket) across all years in the series.

Slots with fewer than Config::minYears valid observations are left as NaN.

Resolution is derived automatically from the median sampling step of the input series. Resolutions finer than Config::maxResolutionDays (default 1 hour) are rejected with ConfigException.
