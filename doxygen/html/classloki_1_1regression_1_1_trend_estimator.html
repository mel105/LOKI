Detailed Description

Decomposition of a time series into trend, seasonal, and residual components.

Fits the combined model: y = trend(t) + seasonal(t) + residual

where: trend(t) = a0 + a1*t (linear, using LinearRegressor internally) seasonal(t) = sum of K harmonic pairs (using HarmonicRegressor internally)

The two components are estimated jointly in a single LSQ fit, not sequentially. This avoids the bias that arises from removing a seasonal signal before estimating the trend.

After fit(), the decomposed components are accessible as TimeSeries via trend(), seasonal(), and residuals().
