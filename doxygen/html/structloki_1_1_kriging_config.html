Detailed Description

Top-level configuration for the loki_kriging pipeline.

Kriging mode: "temporal" – 1-D Kriging in time (h = |t_i - t_j| in MJD days). Implemented in v1. Suitable for gap filling, interpolation, and short-range prediction on a single station time series. "spatial" – 2-D Kriging over a network of stations (h = Euclidean dist). PLACEHOLDER: structure prepared, not yet implemented. "space_time" – Combined spatio-temporal Kriging. PLACEHOLDER: design reserved for future implementation.

Kriging method: "simple" – known constant mean (mu = knownMean). "ordinary" – unknown local mean, sum-of-weights = 1 constraint. DEFAULT. "universal" – mean is a polynomial trend of degree trendDegree. Drift functions are powers of time: {1, t, t^2, ...}.
