Detailed Description

Result of fitting an ARIMA or SARIMA model.

Coefficients are ordered to match the lag index vectors produced by ArimaFitter::arLags() and ArimaFitter::maLags(): arCoeffs[i] corresponds to lag arLags()[i] maCoeffs[i] corresponds to lag maLags()[i]

For a pure non-seasonal model the lag vectors are simply {1, 2, ..., p} and {1, 2, ..., q}. For SARIMA they follow the multiplicative expansion.

The residuals and fitted values are on the (differenced) series passed to ArimaFitter::fit(), not on the original observations.
