Detailed Description

Complete result of a single-channel SSA decomposition.

Produced by SsaAnalyzer::analyze(). Contains the full SVD output, elementary reconstructed components, w-correlation matrix, and the final grouped reconstructions.

Dimension conventions: n = original series length L = window length (number of columns of trajectory matrix) K = n - L + 1 (number of rows of trajectory matrix) r = min(K, L) (number of non-zero singular values)
