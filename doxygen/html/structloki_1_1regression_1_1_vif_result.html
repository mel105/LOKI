Detailed Description

Variance inflation factors for multicollinearity detection.

VIF_j = 1 / (1 - R^2_j), where R^2_j is the R^2 from regressing column j of the design matrix on all other columns. Computed for each predictor column except the intercept (column 0).

Thresholds: VIF > 5 indicates moderate multicollinearity, VIF > 10 indicates severe multicollinearity.
