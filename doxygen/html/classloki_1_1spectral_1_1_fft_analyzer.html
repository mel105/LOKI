Detailed Description

Computes a single-sided power spectral density via Cooley-Tukey radix-2 FFT, with optional Welch averaging.

Amplitude and phase spectra are computed alongside the PSD in a single pass. For Welch averaging, amplitudes and phases are averaged across segments (phase averaging is circular via complex mean).
