Detailed Description

Orchestrates the full homogenization pipeline for a single time series.

Pipeline order:

  1. GapFiller
  2. OutlierCleaner (pre) – coarse removal on raw series, never on residuals
  3. Deseasonalizer – removes seasonal signal (loki_core)
  4. OutlierCleaner (post) – fine removal on residuals directly, no seasonal passed (already removed in step 3)
  5. MultiChangePointDetector
  6. SeriesAdjuster
