◆ penaltyType

std::string loki::homogeneity::PeltDetectorConfig::penaltyType {"bic"}

Penalty type: "bic" | "aic" | "mbic" | "fixed". "bic" : beta = log(n) * sigma^2 – good general default "aic" : beta = 2 * sigma^2 – more change points than bic "mbic" : beta = 3 * log(n) * sigma^2 – conservative, fewer CPs "fixed": beta = fixedPenalty – manual control
