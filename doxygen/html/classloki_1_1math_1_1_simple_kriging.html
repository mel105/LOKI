Detailed Description

Simple Kriging (known constant mean mu).

System: K * lambda = k(t) Estimate: Z*(t) = mu + lambda^T * (z - mu) Variance: sigma^2(t) = max(nugget, C(0) - k^T * lambda)

Most numerically stable variant. Appropriate when the mean is reliably known – e.g. deseasonalized residuals centred at zero.

Cached: K^{-1} (n x n). LOO uses base class shortcut.

FUTURE: locally varying known mean for spatial non-stationarity.
